Privacy Policy for Barcode Masters

Updated on Date: January 8, 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Email Address: Required for account creation and secure login.
  • Username: Publicly displayed within the game.
  • Password: Securely stored for account authentication.
  • Monsters: Includes monster names, stats, AI-generated images, and other data related to gameplay.
  • Game Interaction Data: Includes game progress, training history, and in-game actions.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Account Management: To create and maintain your account.
  • Monster Generation & AI Images: Generated images for monsters based on barcode data and stats.
  • In-Game Features: For features such as monster collection, training, and battles.
  • Communication: To send important updates and notifications.

3. AI-Generated Content

The game uses AI to generate unique images for your monsters, which are stored and tied to your account.

4. Data Security

We take industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data, but cannot guarantee 100% security.

5. Database & Game Bugs

  • Data Loss: There is a possibility of data loss due to technical issues.
  • Game Interruptions: Temporary interruptions may occur during updates or maintenance.

6. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services (e.g., Firebase) to store and process data. Please review their privacy policies.

7. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

8. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your data.
  • Correction: Update inaccuracies in your data.
  • Deletion: Request account deletion and data removal.
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from promotional emails.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy and will notify you by posting updates on this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at gumbydev@icloud.com.
