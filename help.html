Welcome to Barcode Masters!

What is Barcode Masters?

Barcode Masters is an exciting and innovative game where players collect, train, and battle unique monsters. Each monster is created by extracting DNA from barcodes and QR codes, allowing for endless variety and strategic depth. The ultimate goal is to become the master of all other battlers by training your monsters and competing against other players.

How to Get Started

Creating an Account

Currently, accounts must be created directly by the game developer. If you are interested in testing Barcode Masters and do not have an account, please email gumbydev@icloud.com to request access. Make sure to include your name and any relevant information in your email.

Extracting DNA

Once you have an account, you can begin extracting DNA from barcodes and QR codes. This DNA is used to generate unique monsters that you can train and battle with. To extract DNA:

  1. Open the barcode scanning feature within the game.
  2. Scan a barcode or QR code using your device's camera.
  3. The game will generate a monster based on the scanned code.

Training Your Monsters

Training is a key part of becoming a master. You can train your monsters to improve their stats and abilities:

  1. Go to the "Training" section in the game.
  2. Select the monster you want to train.
  3. Choose the type of training you want to perform (e.g., strength, agility, endurance).
  4. Complete the training tasks to enhance your monster's abilities.

Battling Other Players

Test your skills by battling other players:

  1. Navigate to the "Battle" section.
  2. Choose an opponent from the list of available players.
  3. Select your team of monsters and start the battle.
  4. Use strategic moves and abilities to defeat your opponent and climb the leaderboards.

Feedback and Support

We value your feedback and are constantly working to improve the game. If you encounter any issues or have suggestions, please reach out via email at gumbydev@icloud.com.

Thank you for joining Barcode Masters. Get ready to scan, train, and battle your way to the top!
