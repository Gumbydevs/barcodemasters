The Game

Barcode Masters is an exciting new game where players scan codes to create, collect, train, and battle unique monsters. Each monster is created by extracting DNA from barcodes and QR codes. This generates a unique seed number which directly influences the uniqueness of the monster, allowing for endless variety and strategic depth. The ultimate goal is to become the master of all other battlers by training your monsters and competing in PVP battles. Will you rise to the top of the leaderboards?

Creating Unique Monsters

Once you have an account, you can begin extracting DNA from barcodes and QR codes. This DNA is used to generate unique monsters that you can train and battle with.

DNA Extraction Process:

  1. Make sure you are logged into your account.
  2. Navigate to the "DNA Extractor" within the game.
  3. Prepare for optimal scanning:
    • Surface Stability: Place the barcode or QR code on a flat, stable surface like a table.
    • Lighting: Ensure good, even lighting - avoid shadows and glare.
    • Distance: Hold your device 6-8 inches (15-20cm) away from the code.
    • Camera Stability: Use both hands to hold your device steady, or rest your elbows on the table for extra stability.
  4. Scanning technique:
    • Center the code in the scanning frame
    • Hold completely still for several seconds
    • Keep the code in sharp focus - avoid movement
    • Wait for the confirmation beep/vibration
  5. Important: The scanning process requires extreme steadiness. Even slight movement can prevent successful scanning. If the scan fails:
    • Try again with more stable positioning
    • Ensure the code is well-lit and clearly visible
    • Keep the device as motionless as possible
    • Wait a full 2-3 seconds without moving
  6. Once successfully scanned, the game will generate a unique monster based on the code's DNA sequence.
  7. Your new monster is automatically saved to your collection.
  8. Note: You can only extract DNA from each code once. If you lose your monster, you can rescan the same code to regenerate it.

Scanning Tips:

  • QR codes generally scan more reliably than traditional barcodes
  • Larger codes are easier to scan than very small ones
  • Clean, undamaged codes work best
  • Matte surfaces scan better than glossy ones that might create glare
  • If using a mobile device, turning off auto-rotate can help maintain stability

Training Your Monsters

Training is a key part of becoming a master. You can train your monsters to improve their stats and abilities. Note that training time increases exponentially with higher stats:

  1. Go to the "Training" section in the game.
  2. Select the monster you want to train.
  3. Choose which stat to train (HP, AP, or Speed).
  4. Training duration increases with stat level:
    • Stats 0-5: 15-30 seconds
    • Stats 5-10: 1-2 minutes
    • Stats 10-30: Several minutes
    • Stats 30+: Hours (increases exponentially)
  5. Higher stats require more dedication and patience to improve
  6. Training progress persists even if you close the game

NPC Battles

Test your skills by battling against AI-controlled monsters:

  1. Navigate to the "Battle Monsters" section from the main menu
  2. Select one of your monsters that you want to battle with
  3. Click "Start Battle" to begin a match against an AI opponent
  4. During battle, you can:
    • Attack - Deal damage to your opponent
    • Defend - Reduce incoming damage for one turn
    • Heal - Restore some of your monster's HP
  5. Win battles to earn experience points for your monster
  6. Higher-level monsters have better stats and are stronger in battle
