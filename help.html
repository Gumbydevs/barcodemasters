Welcome to Barcode Masters!

What is Barcode Masters?

Barcode Masters is an exciting new game where players scan codes to create, collect, train, and battle unique monsters. Each monster is created by extracting DNA from barcodes and QR codes. This generates a unique seed number which directly influences the uniqueness of the monster, allowing for endless variety and strategic depth. The ultimate goal is to become the master of all other battlers by training your monsters and competing in PVP battles. Will you rise to the top of the leaderboards?

How to Get Started

Creating an Account

As Barcode Masters is in currently in early development, accounts must be created directly by the game developer. If you are interested in testing Barcode Masters and do not have an account, please email gumbydev@icloud.com to request access. Make sure to include your name, email address and any relevant information in your email.

Creating Unique Monsters

Once you have an account, you can begin extracting DNA from barcodes and QR codes. This DNA is used to generate unique monsters that you can train and battle with.

To extract DNA:

  1. Make sure you are logged into your account.
  2. Navigate to the "DNA Extractor" within the game.
  3. Scan a barcode or QR code using your device's camera.
  4. The game will generate a unique monster based on the scanned code. The monster is automatically saved to your collection.

Training Your Monsters

Training is a key part of becoming a master. You can train your monsters to improve their stats and abilities:

  1. Go to the "Training" section in the game.
  2. Select the monster you want to train.
  3. Choose the type of training you want to perform (e.g., strength, agility, endurance).
  4. Complete the training tasks to enhance your monster's abilities.

Battling Other Players

Test your skills by battling other players:

  1. Navigate to the "PVP Battle" section.
  2. Choose an opponent from the list of available players.
  3. Select your your monsters for the fight and click "start battle".
  4. Use strategic moves and abilities to defeat your opponent and climb the leaderboards.
